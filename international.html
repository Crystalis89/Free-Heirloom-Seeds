You may request one ENVELOPE per year (no parcels) to be sent to destinations outside the USA, that are served by USPS international stamps.

The following rules apply -

  1. The importer, requesting seed, is responsible for knowing & complying with the current requirements to import seeds to your country.
  2. We cannot send seeds from our oversized list outside of the US.
  3. You must follow all the rules/guidelines on our Main Page, as well as this page.
  4. Seeds sent outside of the US are not tracked & sent at your own risk.
  5. Requests that are not accompanied with our suggested minimum donation, must say "no donation" in the email subject line
  6. One international envelope per requestor or address per year.

Suggested minimum donation for International requests is $5 USD per envelope with up to 8 packs of seed that are NOT ON OUR OVERSIZED LIST

Please note, we can't always fit 8 packs in an enevelope. (for example: if you choose all watermelon or radish 4 packs is max per envelope)

International requests are not tracked & sent at your own risk.

We should be able to ship to the following countries without restriction we cannot guarantee the accuracy of this information as your country can change it's rules anytime.

Please be sure to verify & comply with the importation rules of your country before requesting

  • Bahamas
  • Bermuda
  • Canada
  • Iceland
  • Norway

Other countries will likely require at least a phytosanitary certificate to import seeds.

Envelopes will be marked as having seeds in them & will likely be rejected at the border if your country is not on this list.

If you do not see your country feel free to search USPS.com to check if your country is served by an international stamp.

Also check your country for seed importation requirements & restrictions.

We would love to expand this list.
