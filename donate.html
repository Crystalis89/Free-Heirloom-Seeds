Your donations & support make this possible!

Many ways you can help!

  1. - Donate seeds! If you have seeds to donate you may send them to -

    Free Heirloom Seeds c/o Stone Spirits 865#B 8th st., Arcata, CA 95521 Our seed donation guidleines are as follows ~

    1. Open pollinated seeds only please.

    2. Seeds must be dry, cleaned from the rest of the plant, have variety name listed & contain recent germination test results

    3. Please include recent germination rates & date of production. Please sprout test homegrown seed before sending. We won't redistribute untested seeds.

    4. We also ask you to self certify if the seeds were grown with recomended isolation from similar species to avoid cross contamination. Please state the actual known distance of any potentially cross pollinating varieties.

    5. If the above criteria are not met, please do not send seeds to us.

    Feel free to email us if you have any questions about donating seeds.

  3. - Donate time.

    We always need help packing seeds, expanding our reach, artistic input, legal work & much more.

    If you would like to donate your time, please send an email to freeheirloomseeds@gmail.com

  4. Grow seeds out for us!

    We are always looking for people to help produce our next run of seeds.

    It may be easier than you think to make a difference in the world.

    It's as simple as requesting some free seeds & growing only a single variety of a given species(example: 1 type of watermelon)

    It's also inmportant that no one else is growing that variety nearby.

    Once you have produced some seed, give them a germination test & see our instructions above for donating seed.

    Thank you!!

  5. - Get involved with our Species Preservation Initiative!

    It is easier than you can imagine to become the steward of a species.

    Choose a species & commit to keeping it alive

  6. - Share our link & tell your friends!!! Such a simple thing can do so much good. Thank You!!
